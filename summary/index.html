Summary of the party

From the detail of the party you can reach the summary info of this party. Just press the 'Summary' button

As you can see, you get the report of all the participants status and all the expenses and payments of this party. If you press the button 'Sent a Mail' you send this report in a mail. This can be used to share the info or to make a back up of this info
