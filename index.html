App Pourpouse

This app helps track the expenses of a party for a group of friends.Usually each one pays different expenses and in the end the settlement is made.Those who have paid more receive from those who have paid less.The application records the expenses, detailing who has paid and who has enjoyed the expense. And it also helps to make the settlement by suggesting and recording payments.

How to use it:

  1. Open a party
  2. Add participants
  3. Enter Expenses
  4. Enter Payments
  5. Summary
  6. History
  7. Sharing the app
